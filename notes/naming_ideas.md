# Naming ideas

RudiOS - Rudimentary Operating System

DYIX - Do your own Unix

Atlas - Holds up the world in greek mythology, symbolizing a foundational system (AtlasOS is already taken)

Janus - Roman god of beginnings, transitions, and duality, fitting for an OS that bridges hardware and software (JanusOS is already taken)

DagdaOS - Named after the Dagda, a father-figure god in Irish mythology associated with knowledge and strength

EnkiOS - Named after Enki, the Sumerian god of wisdom and creation

NabuOS - Named after Nabu, the Babylonian god of wisdom and writing

OgunOS - Named after Ogun, the Yoruba god of iron and technology

NüwaOS - Named after Nüwa, the Chinese goddess of creation and repair

atumOS - Named after the Egyptian deity atum, who created himself
