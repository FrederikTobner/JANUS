# Naming ideas

RudiOS - Rudimentary Operating System

- Abbreviations: ROS
- Backronyms:
- ROS: Rudimentary Operating System

DYIX - Do your own Unix

- Abbreviations: DYIX
- Backronyms:
- DYIX: Do Your (own) *IX* (IX = Unix-like)

Atlas - Holds up the world in greek mythology, symbolizing a foundational system (AtlasOS is already taken)

- Abbreviations: Atlas, Atl
- Backronyms:
- ATLAS: Architecture-Targeted Learning & Systems
- ATLAS: A Tiny Learning (Operating) System

Janus - Roman god of beginnings, transitions, and duality, fitting for an OS that bridges hardware and software (JanusOS is already taken)

- Abbreviations: Janus, Jan
- Backronyms:
- JANUS: Just A Neat Unix-like System
- JANUS: Jumpstart And Navigate Underlying Systems
- JANUS: Just ANother Unix-like System

Dagda - Named after the Dagda, a father-figure god in Irish mythology associated with knowledge and strength

- Abbreviations: Dagda, Dag
- Backronyms:
- DAGDA: Debuggable Academic Guided Development Architecture
- DAGDA: Developer And Guru’s Debugging Assistant
- DAGDA: Direct, Approachable, Guided Development (for) All

Enki - Named after Enki, the Sumerian god of wisdom and creation

- Abbreviations: Enki, Enk
- Backronyms:
- ENKI: Educational Nucleus Kernel Implementation
- ENKI: Extensible (and) Nimble Kernel Infrastructure
- ENKI: Experimental New Kernel Initiative

Nabu - Named after Nabu, the Babylonian god of wisdom and writing

- Abbreviations: Nabu, Nab
- Backronyms:
- NABU: Native ABI Boot Utility
- NABU: Neat And Brief Unix-like (kernel)
- NABU: New Architecture Bootstrapping Utility

Ogu - Named after Ogun, the Yoruba god of iron and technology

- Abbreviations: Ogu
- Backronyms:
- OGU: Operating-system Groundwork Unit
- OGU: OS Growth Utility
- OGU: Onboard (system) Getting-Started Utility

Nüwa - Named after Nüwa, the Chinese goddess of creation and repair

- Abbreviations: Nuwa, Nuw
- Backronyms:
- NUWA: New Userland Workshop Architecture
- NUWA: Nucleus Utilities, Working (and) Approachable
- NUWA: New World Assembler (to kernel)

atum - Named after the Egyptian deity atum, who created himself

- Abbreviations: Atum, ATUM
- Backronyms:
- ATUM: Archaic Trivial User Manager
- ATUM: Architecture-Targeted Utility Microkernel
- ATUM: Async Task + Utility Manager
